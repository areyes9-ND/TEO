Why TEO Exists

TEO was founded to create a collaborative network for student-run organizations and to fill gaps that traditional counseling services may leave. We believe that peer-driven support creates unique value that complements professional guidance.

From the Founder, Minnow: "A lot of smaller non-profits are made by high schoolers, and this platform provides a space where smaller organizations can partner up. This is a collaborative program where students can come together and cover niches that counselors or CBOs may overlook. TEO is a free resource platform with insights from personal experiences."
